Our Services

How We Can Help

When social services become involved with your family, the stakes could not be higher. We provide expert legal representation to parents and family members at every stage.

Public Law Outline (PLO) Meetings

Understanding the concerns raised by social services, addressing them proactively, and working to avoid court proceedings wherever possible. Early intervention can make all the difference.

Care Orders & Supervision Orders

Robust representation when the local authority applies for care or supervision orders. We challenge evidence, instruct experts, and fight for your children to remain with you.

Emergency Protection Orders (EPOs)

Urgent representation when a child has been or may be removed. We act quickly to challenge emergency applications and protect your rights as a parent.

Interim Care Hearings

Working to keep children with parents or relatives during proceedings. We advocate for the least restrictive placement and ensure assessments are fair and thorough.

Special Guardianship Applications

Helping grandparents, aunts, uncles, and other relatives provide long-term care through Special Guardianship Orders. We guide you through the application and assessment process.

Legal Aid funding is available for eligible parents and family members in care proceedings. You may qualify for free legal representation — contact us today to check your eligibility and get the support you need.
