Criminal Defence

From the police station to the courtroom, we provide robust and strategic defence. Over 40 years' combined experience protecting your rights and freedom.

Our Services

How We Can Help

We provide expert criminal defence across a wide range of offences, from initial police station advice through to Crown Court trials and appeals.

Serious & Complex Crime

GBH, violent offences, firearms, county lines, and organised crime. We handle the most serious charges with a meticulous, strategic approach to your defence.

Fraud & Financial Crime

Fraud, money laundering, benefit fraud, and regulatory breaches. Our team understands the complexities of financial crime and will fight to protect your interests.

Drug Offences

Possession, supply, conspiracy, and trafficking. Whether you face minor possession charges or serious trafficking allegations, we provide robust representation.

Driving Offences

Speeding, no insurance, drink/drug driving, and dangerous driving. We work to protect your licence and minimise the impact on your life and livelihood.

Sexual Offences

Historic offences, safeguarding matters, and false accusations. We handle these sensitive cases with the utmost discretion and a thorough, evidence-based defence.

Youth Crime

Specialist representation for young people in Magistrates' and Youth Courts. We understand the unique legal framework and focus on rehabilitation and the best outcomes.

Police Station Representation

24/7 emergency attendance at police stations across London and the South East. Early legal advice can make a critical difference to the outcome of your case.

Appeals & Post-Conviction

Conviction and sentence appeals, Criminal Cases Review Commission (CCRC) applications. If you believe there has been a miscarriage of justice, we can help.

Accredited Duty Solicitors available 24/7. We are committed to providing immediate legal support whenever you need it. Legal Aid is available where eligible — contact us to check your eligibility.
